Søften [ alternative spellings range from Suten(1386), Suchten (1399) and Søwten (1489)] is a small Danish town in Jutland, roughly three kilometers south of Hinnerup and just northwest of Aarhus. Søften has a population of 2,722 (1.January 2014).[1] Archaeological surveys of the area (2), indicate the earliest settlements dates to the Nordic Iron Age.
