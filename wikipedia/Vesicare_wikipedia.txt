Solifenacin (INN, trade name Vesicare) is a medicine of the antimuscarinic class and was developed for treating contraction of overactive bladder[1] with associated problems such as increased urination frequency and urge incontinence.[2] It is manufactured and marketed by Astellas, GlaxoSmithKline[3] and Teva Pharmaceutical Industries.[citation needed]
