Clopidogrel, sold under the brand name Plavix among others, is a medication that is used to reduce the risk of heart disease and stroke in those at high risk. It is also used together with aspirin in heart attacks and following the placement of a coronary artery stent. It is taken by mouth.[1] Onset of effects is about 2 hours and lasts for 5 days[1]
