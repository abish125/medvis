gastroenterology gas·tro·en·ter·ol·o·gy (gās'trō-ěn'tə-rŏl'ə-jē)n.  The medical specialty concerned with the function and disorders of the stomach, intestines, and related organs of the gastrointestinal tract. gas'tro·en'ter·o·log'ic (-ə-lŏj'ĭk) or gas'tro·en'ter·o·log'i·cal adj.
