Hydrocodone/paracetamol, also known as hydrocodone/acetaminophen or hydrocodone/APAP and marketed under the trade name Vicodin among others, is the combination of an opioid pain medication, hydrocodone, with paracetamol (acetaminophen). It is used to relieve moderate to severe pain.[1] It exists in tablet, elixir and solution in various strengths for oral administration.[1]
