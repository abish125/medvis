Loratadine, sold under the brand name Claritin among others, is a medication used to treat allergies. This includes allergic rhinitis (hay fever) and hives. It is also avaliable in combination with pseudoephedrine, a decongestant, known as loratadine/pseudoephedrine. It is taken by mouth.[1]
