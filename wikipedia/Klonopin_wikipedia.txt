Clonazepam, sold under the brand name Klonopin among others, is a medication used to prevent and treat seizures, panic disorder, and for the movement disorder known as akathisia.[3] It is a tranquilizer of the benzodiazepine class. It is taken by mouth.[3] It begins having an effect within an hour and lasts between six and 12 hours.[2]
