The domestic dog (Canis lupus familiaris) is a usually furry, carnivorous[2][3][4] canid carnivoran mammal. Domestic dogs are commonly known as "man's best friend". The dog was the first domesticated animal[5][6] and has been widely kept as a working, hunting, and pet companion. It is estimated there are between 700 million and one billion domestic dogs, making them the most abundant member of order Carnivora.[7][8]
