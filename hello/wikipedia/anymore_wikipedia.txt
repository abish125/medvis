"Anymore" is the title of a song co-written and recorded by American country music artist Travis Tritt. It was released in September 1991 as the second single from his album It's All About to Change. It peaked at #1 in both the United States and Canada, becoming his second number-one hit in the United States, and his fourth number-one in Canada.[1] The song was written by Tritt and Jill Colucci.
