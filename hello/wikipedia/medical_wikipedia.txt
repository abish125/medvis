Medicine (UK English i/ˈmɛdsɨn/, /ˈmɛdɨsɨn/; US English i/ˈmɛdɨsɨn/) is the science or practice of the diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of disease.[1][2] The word medicine is derived from the Latin ars medicina, meaning the art of healing.[3][4] Medicine encompasses a variety of health care practices evolved to maintain and restore health by the prevention and treatment of illness.
